ABOUT ME - JADE

I'm Jade, the designated chill creative of BSCS 102! I have a laid-back personality and consider myself a master of embracing the nonchalant life—especially when it comes to getting extra sleep. Coffee is my non-negotiable fuel for creativity!

I am a multifaceted creative who finds solace in the quiet hours of editing, dancing to my own beat, and freezing moments in time through photography. I also love playing musical instruments, which lets me find rhythm outside of code.

Solitude is my happy place. It’s where I can truly listen, observe the world around me, and let my creative ideas flourish before bringing them back to the team.
